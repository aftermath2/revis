# Revis

Revis is a platform based on nostr where users can review notes and commit to their opinions by putting their sats where their mouse is.

The two pillars this project is based on are:

- **Bitcoin**: enables energy-backed reviews thanks to proof-of-work.
- **Nostr**: enables decentralized, censorship-resistance public communications.

## Core Concepts

- **Notes**: The subjects being reviewed (products, services, books, proposals, etc.)
- **Reviews**: User-submitted evaluations with a rating and comment
- **Zaps**: Lightning payments that back and weight reviews

## Energy-backed Opinions

When you write a review, you don't just type words, you attach real value to them.

Not all reviews carry equal weight. A review backed by 21,000 sats influences the rating more than one backed by 21 sats, effectively prioritizing those who have more energy channeled to them.

Apart from reviewing notes, you can also zap other users' reviews.

The zaps on reviews act as a signal of quality and conviction. High-zapped reviews float to the top, while low-effort opinions fade.
